Information

Stak suite is a collection of pentesting/ethical hacking tools made for the Stak CTF team.
If you are unfamiliar with the term "CTF" (Capture The Flag), it is essentially a competition where players attempt to collect "flags" and submit them for points. These flags are usually hidden in vulnerable websites or programs.
It could also be used to help developers test for vulnerabilities on other websites, or just for fun/ease of access.
Currently only cryptography tools are provided, but much more will be implemented in the future.
